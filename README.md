A $25M specialty food brand is leaving $1.4M&#8211;$3.1M on the table every year. The losses hide across ten operating decisions nobody is making on purpose &#8212; SKU rationalization, product data health, deduction recovery, fulfillment accuracy, EDI compliance, channel profitability, revenue lifecycle, retail readiness, launch economics, and weekly operational discipline.

That framework &#8212; [The Ten Decisions](https://lailarallc.com/the-ten-decisions) &#8212; organizes everything here. Each repo is a worked example: synthetic data, real methodology, a deliverable built for someone who needs to act on what's in it.

### Free tools

No login, no cost. Built for the founder who needs an answer before the engagement starts.

- **[Retail Readiness Scorecard](https://msshawnp.github.io/retail-readiness-scorecard/)** &#8212; 12&#8211;18 questions, Red/Yellow/Green across eight dimensions, downloadable PDF
- **[GTIN Validator](https://gtin.lailarallc.com)** &#8212; product data validation against GS1 standards with retailer-specific context
- **[EDI Preflight](https://edi.lailarallc.com)** &#8212; parse 850s, validate 856s against Walmart/Amazon/UNFI/KeHE/Costco specs
- **[Cost of Saying Yes](https://launch-cost.lailarallc.com)** &#8212; month-by-month cash flow model for a major retailer launch
- **[Monday Morning Report](https://github.com/MsShawnP/monday-morning-report)** &#8212; weekly Excel template, three numbers, scaled to your revenue tier
- **[Data Differences Tool](https://diff.lailarallc.com)** &#8212; compare two spreadsheets, get a clear report of what changed
